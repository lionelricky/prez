Introduction

Addressing the problems.

In it's quest to grow as a business, DT Design would like to modernize their systems. This modernization would allow DT Design to be more effective, organized and flexible. Identified goals include:

  • Switch from paper to digital records, invoices and contracts.
  • Store records, invoices and contracts in a database.
  • Enable clients to submit requests remotely and securely.
  • Enable employees to create invoices, contracts and records securely.
  • Create an intranet space for employees (employee directory, post events, company news, etc.).
  • Create a company website for the general public.
