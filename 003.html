Proposal

A global online solution

To meet the needs of DTDesign, I suggest a global online solution divided into 3 phases:

  • Phase 1: Creation of Website for general public.
  • Phase 2: Creation of Internal Intranet which will contain employee section and administrative section with applications for creating/sending quotes, contracts and invoicing.
  • Phase 3: Creation of Client section for registration and quote requests.

This solution takes into consideration:

  • Company size.
  • Reduce costs of development
  • Promote ease of maintenance
